Non-selective monoamine oxidase (MAO) inhibitors - including phenelzine sulfate, tranylcypromine sulfate and pargyline HC1. Concomitant use of L-phenylalanine and non-selective MAO inhibitors may cause hypertension. Selegiline - L-phenylalanine and the selective MAO inhibitor selegiline may have synergistic antidepressant activity if used concomitantly. Neuroleptic Drugs - L-phenylalanine may potentiate the tardive dyskinesia side reactions of neuroleptic drugs if used concomitantly with them.
