Interactions  Interaction may occur with Antacids, Calcium supplements, Cholestyramine, Choline and magnesium salicylates, Colestipol, Iron-containing drugs, Laxatives, Magnesium salicylate: these drugs may decrease the potency of tetracyclines. Oral contraceptives containing estrogen: tetracyclines may decrease the contraceptive effect and increase the chance of unwanted pregnancy.
