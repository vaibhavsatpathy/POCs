Drug Interactions:  Pirenzepine may interact with the following drugs, which may affect the effect of Pirenzepine or whose effects may be affected. - Antacids - Diarrhea medicine containing kaolin or attapulgite - Ketoconazole - Central nervous system (CNS) depressants - Other anticholinergics - Tricyclic antidepressants (e.g., Elavil, Asendin, Anafranil, Pertofrane, Sinequan, Tofranil, Aventyl, Surmontil) - Potassium chloride (e.g., Kay Ciel)
