Some anticonvulsants may interact with Mephenytoin. They can either increase or decrease the effect of Mephenytoin. Those anticonvulsants include divalproex sodium, valproic acid, and phenobarbital. Mephenytoin may also affect the effects of other drugs, which include some steroid medications, warfarin, certain heart medicines, birth control pills, anti-infective medicines, furosemide and theophylline Please note that Mephenytoin may interact with other drugs that are not listed here.
