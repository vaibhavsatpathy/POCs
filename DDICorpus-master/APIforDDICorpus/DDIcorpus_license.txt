2. Texts

The texts contained in the corpus are from MedLine, a database of the 
U.S. National Library of Medicine (NLM). Please see the NLM page on copyright information regarding 
the copyright of the abstracts: http://www.nlm.nih.gov/databases/download.html. 

Moreover, the corpus also contains texts from previous versions of the pharmacological database www.drugbank.ca, 
which were gathered from the textual field about drug interactions. It should be noted that this field is not available in the current version of drugbank.
